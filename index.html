NEO Burlesque

Heading

Tradtional Burlesque was defined by satirical skits of popular literature as well as striptease and elaborate dance performances. Neo Burlesque takes it up a notch by allowing an anything goes approach to perfomance.

Enter Glitterotica a Neo Burlesque Troope based in San Luis Obispo. This year's their having a Valentine's Day show. With ten acts and twelve performers, the troupe prepared for over four weeks to prepare.
